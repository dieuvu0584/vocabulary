Privacy Policy

Vocabulary — Vocab AI  |  Last updated: April 16, 2025

🔒 This app is designed with privacy first. All your learning data is stored locally on your device. We do not collect, store, or sell any personal information.

1. Overview

Vocabulary (Vocab AI) is a multilingual vocabulary learning application developed by an independent developer (SMA). This Privacy Policy explains what information the app accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding that information.

By using this app, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information. All data generated by your use of the app is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.

The following data is stored locally on your device only:

  • Vocabulary learning progress (words learned, review schedule, ratings)
  • App settings (selected language, theme, notification preferences)
  • AI API keys you choose to enter (encrypted via Android Keystore)

3. Permissions Used

Permission Why it's needed
POST_NOTIFICATIONS To send daily vocabulary review reminders (opt-in only)
SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM
USE_EXACT_ALARM		 To schedule daily reminders at the exact time you set
RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED To reschedule reminders after the device restarts
REQUEST_IGNORE_BATTERY_OPTIMIZATIONS To ensure reminder notifications are delivered reliably
WAKE_LOCK To keep the screen on during auto-play vocabulary sessions

None of these permissions are used to collect or share personal data.

4. AI Features & Third-Party API

The app includes an optional AI-powered chat assistant. To use this feature, you may enter your own API key from a third-party AI provider (such as OpenAI, Google Gemini, etc.).

  • Your API key is stored encrypted on your device only using Android Keystore / flutter_secure_storage.
  • When you use the AI chat feature, your messages (vocabulary questions) are sent directly from your device to the AI provider's servers under their own privacy policy.
  • We do not have access to your API key or your chat history.
  • Using AI features is entirely optional. The app works fully offline without them.

5. In-App Purchases

The app offers optional premium features through Google Play In-App Purchases. All payment transactions are processed by Google Play and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy. We do not receive or store any payment card information.

6. Text-to-Speech (TTS)

The app uses on-device text-to-speech to pronounce vocabulary words. This feature runs entirely on your device and no audio data is sent to external servers.

7. Data Storage & Security

  • All vocabulary data and progress are stored in a local SQLite database on your device.
  • App settings are stored in SharedPreferences (local storage).
  • Sensitive data (API keys) is encrypted using flutter_secure_storage (backed by Android Keystore).
  • No data is backed up to external servers by this app.
